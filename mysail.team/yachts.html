Mysail.team is the ultimate crew management tool for racing yachts, helping yacht owners and crew managers easily manage their crew and connect with new sailors.

Save Time

Save time with simple scheduling and communication features.

Schedule races, training, work days and social events with the push of a button. Invite your entire crew or individuals members and view responses all in one place.

Improve Communication

Easily communicate with your entire crew or individual members with social messaging.

See who's viewed messages and keep crew accountable with a messaging function visible to the entire crew, or easily send individual messages with the push of a button.

Eliminate Frustrations

Reduce time spent chasing crew with automated communication.

If a crewmember hasn't responded in the allocated time, Mysail will automatically send them a reminder to respond. In the lead-up to race day, all participating crew will get an automatic reminder with any important race-day details.

Gain Visibility

Quickly view crew commitments, positions and availability in the calendar view.

Easily see if you are short crew for a race and find out who else is available with the required skills. If you don't have any available crewmembers in your roster to fill the position, you can search for available crew matching your needs with the push of a button.

Find Crew

Never sail short-handed again (unless, of course, you want to), by easily connecting with new sailors matched to the needs of your yacht.

If you're looking for new crewmembers for an individual race or on an ongoing basis, Mysail will match you with crewmembers based on your commitment, social and skill requirements. By promoting your yacht and advertising positions, crew can even come to you!

Gain Confidence

Reduce uncertainty of taking new crew members on-board with ratings and reviews.

See how crew have performed in the past and find out their skill level for each position with our simple and intuitive rating and review system. You can leave a review for all new sailors that come on-board, and they can do the same for you, helping to promote your yacht to new potential crewmembers.

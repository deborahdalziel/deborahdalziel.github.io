Khaleesi Sailing

Khaleesi Sailing team has been competing in the Australian racing scene since August 2014. The yacht is a DK46 and is crewed by a team of dedicated amateur sailors who push her to achieve results, while having fun along the way.

Our Program

Khaleesi participates in a range of events from twilight and club races through to national regattas and major offshore events.

For the 2016 season Khaleesi is competing in the Bluewater pointscore which starts in July with the Sydney to Gold Coast race, includes 4 shorter offshore events and conculudes with the iconic Sydney to Hobart race.

Khaleesi is will also be participating in the 2016 National IRC championships being held in Hamilton Island in August.

The Team

Owned by Andrew and Pauline Dally, Khalessi's crew is made up of a dedicated group of amateur sailors.

Most of the crew have been sailing together since the team's formation in August 2014, with the occasional newbie or visitor welcomed along the way. With a diverse range of backgrounds and experience, the team members are united by a common love of sailing and foucs on getting results.

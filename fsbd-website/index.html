About us

At Financial Solutions by Design, we focus on designing mortgage solutions that help Australians achieve their property dreams.

Led by industry veteran Scott Compton, we are a boutique mortgage brokerage located in North Sydney.

Contact us today to find out how our expertise and exceptional customer service can make your next home loan experience headache-free.

Scott Compton

Director Scott Compton has over 15 years experience in the home loan industry.

Scott works with his clients to establish lending solutions that meet their short, medium and long-term goals, assisting with the entire process from application submission to post settlement support.

His passion for helping people achieve their property goals drives Scott to provide the best mortgage solutions for each and every client.

What we do

Whether you're a first time home buyer, wealth accumulator, retiree or simply looking to consolidate your home loan and personal debts, we can provide advice and assistance to help you every step of the way.

We're here for you...

At Financial Solutions by Design, we understand that a sharp interest rate is important. However, this shouldn't be the only consideration when looking at your home loan options.

We take the time to truly understand your short, medium and long-term home loan needs so we can recommend a solution that's right for you.

...from start to finish...

We will ensure your entire home loan experience is simple and headache-free.

As well as helping with the loan submission and approval process, we ensure your home loan and day-to-day banking needs are set up correctly.

...with the expertise you need...

Working closely with financial advisers and accountants, Financial Solutions by Design have extensive experience dealing with complex income and home loan structures.

Our expertise encompasses self-employed borrowers, trusts and Self-Managed Super Funds. Plus, we can help with business loans and development finance.

...for long term success.

In this competitive lending environment, if you haven't had your home loan reviewed in the past 12 months you are probably paying too much.

As part of our exceptional service we will continue to review your loan to ensure your home loan structure and rate are still working for you.
